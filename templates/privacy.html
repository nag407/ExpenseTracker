Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly collects, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your data. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information such as pages visited and actions taken, used to improve the app.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain your Spendly account.
  • Display your expense history and spending summaries.
  • Send essential account-related communications (e.g. password resets).
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the service.

We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell it to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, alteration, or deletion.

While we take security seriously, no system is completely immune to risk. You are responsible for keeping your account password confidential and for any activity that occurs under your account.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal information and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or using the account deletion option in your profile settings.

6. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services (such as hosting providers) to operate the platform. These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and only as needed to perform their services.

We do not share your expense data or personal details with any third party for marketing or analytics purposes.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
